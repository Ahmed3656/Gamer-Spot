Game Genres

RPG

A game in which each participant assumes the role of a character that can interact within the game's imaginary world

Fantasy

A game in which players assume the roles of characters and act out fantastical adventures, the outcomes of which are partially determined by chance, as by the roll of dice

Puzzle

Focuses on completion, which requires players to solve a logic puzzle or navigate a challenge to progress to the next, more difficult challenge

Multiplayer

Designed specifically to have people interact with each other via their avatars or in-game characters

Strategy

A game (e.g. a board game) in which the players' uncoerced, and often autonomous, decision-making skills have a high significance in determining the outcome

Sports

They are usually based on real-world sports, but can also be fictional or exaggerated
